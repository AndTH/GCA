Welcome to PIC-MINI-WEB development board!

PIC-MINI-WEB development board is used to demonstrate the power and abilities of an 8-bit embedded web server. This board is produced by Olimex Ltd. 

You can push the on-board button (BUTTON).  You may also click on one of the two command buttons to toggle the on-board led (STAT) or extension port PD6.

More information about this board you can find here http://www.olimex.com/dev/pic-mini-web.html.
